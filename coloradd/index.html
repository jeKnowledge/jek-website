WHAT WE DO

CHANGING LIVES

5% of the world population is color blind. That means 350 million people struggle in their everyday routines due to having no access to help or alternatives to the traditional universal color system.

Color is used in everything and we believe it should be for all...
...this is why we created ColorADD.
